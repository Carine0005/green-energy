Community Solar Programs

Community solar allows people to benefit from solar energy without paying the full cost of installing rooftop panels. Families can subscribe to a shared solar project and receive credits on their electricity bills. This lowers upfront costs and makes clean energy accessible to renters, condo owners, and households with tighter budgets.
